Services

Complete Car Care Under One Roof

From essential maintenance to complex mechanical work, our service bays are equipped for hatchbacks, sedans, SUVs and commercial vehicles.

Computer Diagnostics

We plug directly into your vehicle’s ECU using professional‑grade scanners to read live data, error codes and sensor performance.

  • Check‑engine light inspection
  • ABS / airbag system scan
  • Battery & alternator health check

Periodic Maintenance

Scheduled servicing every 10,000 km keeps your engine lubricated, filters clean and warranty valid, improving fuel economy and life.

  • Engine oil & filter replacement
  • Air, fuel & cabin filter cleaning
  • All‑fluid top‑up & 40‑point check

Brakes, Tyres & Suspension

Stopping power and road grip are critical. We measure pad thickness, rotor run‑out and tyre tread depth before recommending parts.

  • Ceramic brake pad & disc change
  • Wheel alignment & balancing
  • Shock absorber & bush replacement

AC & Cooling System

We restore cabin comfort by checking refrigerant pressure, leaks, condenser condition and coolant circulation to prevent overheating.

  • AC gas recharge & leak detection
  • Radiator flush & thermostat check
  • Water pump & coolant hose repair
